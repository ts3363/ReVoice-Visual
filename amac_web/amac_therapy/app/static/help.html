üìö Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a therapy session?
Navigate to the Therapy section from the dashboard. Choose an exercise type and click the microphone button to start recording. Speak clearly into your microphone and the AI will analyze your pronunciation and provide real-time feedback.
What types of exercises are available?
AMAC Therapy offers various exercise types including: Pronunciation Practice (individual words and phrases), Reading Exercises (paragraphs and stories), Tongue Twisters (for articulation), and Conversation Practice (dialogue scenarios). Each exercise is designed to target different aspects of speech improvement.
How is my progress tracked?
Your progress is automatically tracked after each session. Visit the Progress page to see detailed analytics including: accuracy trends over time, monthly activity charts, session history, and personalized improvement suggestions. You can also export your progress data as PDF or CSV.
Is my voice data secure?
Yes! We take privacy seriously. Voice recordings are processed securely and are only used for analysis during your session. Your personal progress data is stored securely and is only accessible to you. We do not share your data with third parties.
What microphone should I use?
For best results, use a good quality microphone in a quiet environment. Most built-in laptop microphones work fine, but a USB headset or external microphone will provide clearer audio and better analysis results. Make sure to grant microphone permissions when prompted.
Can I use AMAC Therapy on mobile?
AMAC Therapy is designed to be responsive and works on most mobile browsers. However, for the best experience with voice recording and analysis, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer with a stable internet connection.
